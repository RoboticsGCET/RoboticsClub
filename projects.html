Projects in Club


Our talented club members have prototyped over 20+ projects, spanning various innovative and impactful ideas. Here are some of the best ones.

Drone

Developed an autonomous drone for surveying agricultural fields using image recognition.

Greet Robot

Created a humanoid robot capable of performing basic tasks and interacting with humans.

Snake Robot

A Snake Robot is a flexible, multi-jointed robot that moves like a snake to navigate tight or tricky spaces.

Spider Robot

A Spider Robot is a multi-legged robot designed to walk over uneven surfaces.

Automatic braking Vehicle

A Vehicle that can stop itself.

Obstacle Detector

A device that detects obstacles in the path of a moving object.
